With the CRP, Westpac has standardised its approach to detecting and resolving fraud and other misconduct breaches, and has ensured consistent follow up and closure of alerts, reporting integrity, and a full audit trail.

The CRP project originated as a lightweight project to serve a small number of low risk portfolios within the Financial Crime Management division.
